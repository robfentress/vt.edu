Register for a Campus Visit

There will be no information sessions or campus tours from August 10th - August 19th, 2018.

When would you like to visit?


PLEASE READ !!

Visit sessions are added on a continuous basis. If no months are currently available, all visits in that month are filled to capacity. Please check again at a later date for newly scheduled sessions.

NOTE: Smaller party sizes are easier to accommodate.

In the meantime, you may still come to visit and participate in an information session and take a self-guided tour. These activities require no prior registration.

 

Information sessions are Monday and Friday at 8:30 a.m., 10:30 a.m., and 1:30 p.m.; Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday at 8:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m..
