You are considered to be a transfer applicant if you have attempted 12 or more credit hours at another college or university any time following graduation from high school.

Virginia Tech Courses and Transferable VCCS Equivalents

Your transcripts will be evaluated for transfer credit after you accept Virginia Tech's offer of admission.

The office of the dean of the college, or in some cases the department into which you transfer, is responsible for this evaluation and for determining transfer credit awards. Keep this in mind when using the list of Virginia Tech courses and VCCS transferable equivalents.

NOTE -- Individual community colleges may not offer all of the courses listed. Please check with your individual college to see if specific courses are offered.
