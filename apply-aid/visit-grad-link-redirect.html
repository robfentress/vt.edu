                                                        Take an Online Virtual Tour

                                                        Meet Your Department

                                                        Contact the department or departments that you’re interested in to set up a meeting while you’re here on campus. They can introduce you to faculty members you’d be studying with and give you details about what it’s like to be a student in their programs.

                                                        Contact your department directly, or we’re happy to help you set up a meeting (but ask that you give us one week’s notice).

                                                        • Find contact information by department
                                                        • Email grads@vt.edu for assistance

                                                        Virginia Tech Visitor’s Center

                                                        Find information about parking passes, maps, accessibility, where to stay, and sights to see while you’re here.

                                                        Extended Campus Locations

                                                        In addition to the main Blacksburg campus, you can also visit our extended campus locations, where we offer certain degree programs and graduate certificates.

                                                        Tour the Graduate School

                                                        Join us for a guided tour of campus. Request a tour.

                                                        Tours start in the Graduate Life Center lobby and last about one hour, including a van tour of campus and the local Blacksburg area. Please allow one week for us to arrange your visit.

                                                        • Group Tour Sign up
