Pre-Professional Programs

Looking to start a career in law, education, or medical studies? Pre-professional studies offer a wealth of options. Whatever destination you’re looking for, degrees in these programs of study can help start you on the right track. Opportunities in undergraduate research, hands-on learning, and real-world experience are ample throughout the university. While these are popular options, they are certainly not the only path to follow. Many pre-professional objectives can start in any major.

The lists below are not complete and only show a handful of majors that some students choose within the area.

For more information on all options, contact the appropriate department or college.
