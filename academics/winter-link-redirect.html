For students who need to catch up, or want to get ahead, Winter Session is just like fall and spring sessions, but is condensed into a three week period over winter break. Winter Session is a perfect opportunity if you are double majoring, planning on graduating early, or simply want to lighten your course load for spring or fall. Classes do fill up, so remember to register for classes soon. Students may register for up to 6 credits during Winter Session.

ONLINE COURSES

These classes are 100 percent online, done on your time.

ON-CAMPUS COURSES

Prefer to stay in Blacksburg? Winter Session has you covered.

BLENDED COURSES

Just as they sound, these classes are a combination of online and in-person elements.

WINTER ABROAD

We know you are a jet setter, so check out the winter abroad course options.
