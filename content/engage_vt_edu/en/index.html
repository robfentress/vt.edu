Kick off your year with service!

Virginia Tech's center for service-learning, leadership education, & civic engagement

At VT Engage, we partner with nonprofit organizations to create service programs based on community need, work with students to develop their leadership competencies, and engage with faculty on community learning opportunities. Our experiences are open to all Hokie students regardless of major or year.
