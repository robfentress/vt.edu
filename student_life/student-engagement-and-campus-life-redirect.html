      Student Engagement and Campus Life

      Gobblerfest - August 31, 2018

      Student Engagement and Campus Life complements the academic experience by building communities, promoting holistic education and cultivating environments where lived experiences can occur through leadership, innovation, and service. As we recognize the diverse needs of each student, we foster collaboration with strategic partners to provide excellence in personal development, customer service, and opportunities for growth.

      Student Engagement and Campus Life is Hiring!

      We are looking for smart, dedicated students to join our team. Working with SECL allows student employees opportunities to learn customer service, critical thinking, teamwork, organization and planning, and helping a large department to fulfill its mission of creating a vibrant campus culture through engagement and involvement. Please send all candidates to the SECL Pool unless otherwise noted.
