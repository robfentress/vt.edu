Welcome Virginia Tech students!

Career and Professional Development works with all Virginia Tech students: all majors; all academic levels, first-year through graduate level; all career topics. Our advisors help students explore career options, seek experience of many kinds including internships and co-ops, seek post-graduation employment, and plan for graduate or professional school.

Wherever you are in your career journey, we can help you on your way.

For liberal arts & social science majors:
Current Jobs for Graduates includes internships too.

For all internship-seeking and job-seeking students: CareerShift is a comprehensive search tool for every career field.

For all students seeking internships or jobs: GoinGlobal tells you the skills and gives you resources to focus your search abroad or in the U.S.

For all students who want to learn about careers: Candid Career gives you quick video interviews of people working in real jobs.

For all internship-seeking and job-seeking students: Career Spots gives you quick videos about job search skills and tips.

Books for all career interests! Pursue your unique interests, discover and follow your passions! In our Career Resource Center choose from hundreds of books you can read [and check out] covering all career fields.
